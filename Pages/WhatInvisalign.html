What Are Invisalign Braces?

Invisalign® is a revolutionary method for straightening teeth without the brackets and wires of traditional braces. Initially developed by a college student seeking a better solution for minor alignment issues, Invisalign received FDA approval in 1999. Since then, Align Technology has continuously enhanced the product and broadened the capabilities of Invisalign treatment. Initially more expensive than braces, Invisalign's cost has now become comparable due to advancements and increased accessibility.

Exploring The Bottom Line — Invisalign Cost Vs. Braces Cost

The national average cost for Invisalign treatment typically ranges between $3,000 and $8,000. Several factors influence the price, such as geographic location and whether the treatment is conducted in an urban or rural area. This cost covers the entire treatment plan from start to finish, including all aligner trays and necessary exams. At Pitner & Carroll Orthodontics, our pricing remains competitive within these ranges and may even be slightly lower. Other factors that can affect the cost of Invisalign treatment include the complexity of the case, the duration of treatment, and any additional orthodontic services required.

    How Does Invisalign Work?

    Traditional braces work by using tension from the archwire to pull teeth into alignment. In contrast, Invisalign utilizes gentle pressure applied against the teeth to gradually push them into new positions. This gentle and constant pressure is typically more comfortable than braces. Moreover, the nearly invisible tray aligners offer a discreet treatment method, allowing you to undergo orthodontic treatment without drawing attention to your teeth.

    The Invisalign treatment involves sets of trays, each set containing an upper and lower tray. You wear each tray set for one to two weeks before progressing to the next set in the sequence. It's important to wear the trays for at least 20 to 22 hours daily, but they can be removed for eating, drinking, and oral hygiene, providing flexibility and convenience during treatment.

    Invisalign Process — How We Make Plastic Magic For Your Mouth

    Invisalign is entirely customized for each individual's mouth! Dr. Pitner follows a meticulous process to create your custom tray aligners, ensuring high quality and a perfect fit. This personalized approach guarantees predictable results without disrupting your daily lifestyle.

    The first step in creating your Invisalign treatment plan is to generate a 3D map of your mouth. This model is manipulated using computer-aided design (CAD) software to devise a personalized treatment plan. Previously, this process could take several weeks with manually created tray models, but with advancements such as intraoral scanners and digital imaging, it now only takes a few minutes. This technological advancement has significantly streamlined the treatment planning process for greater efficiency and accuracy.

    We can create your customized treatment plan using the 3D model of your mouth. With the ClinCheck® software for Invisalign, we meticulously map out each movement of your teeth. This advanced technology allows you to visualize your final smile before you even receive your first set of trays, giving you a clear understanding of your treatment journey and expected results.

    The Equipment And Materials

    Digital scanning, imaging equipment, and advanced software seamlessly integrate thousands of images into a single 3D model of your mouth. The scanner operates at an impressive speed, capturing up to 6,000 images per second! Once Dr. Pitner completes the scan, you'll be able to view a detailed 3D image of your mouth on the computer screen. Remarkably, this entire process is completed in just a few minutes, providing efficient and precise data for your Invisalign treatment plan.

    We will create a personalized treatment plan tailored specifically to your needs. Depending on the level of alignment correction required, your treatment may involve just a few sets of trays or up to 70 or more. Each set of trays applies gentle yet precise pressure to your teeth, guiding them gradually into their desired positions. As you progress from one set to the next, you may feel a subtle adjustment in where the pressure is focused, indicating the progress of your treatment.

    Invisalign utilizes a proprietary polymer known as SmartTrack® material, which is exclusive to Invisalign aligner trays. Researchers spent over eight years testing more than 260 materials to develop this advanced polymer. When used in conjunction with SmartForce® attachments, Invisalign aligners provide comparable effectiveness to traditional braces. The durability of SmartTrack material enables Invisalign to effectively address both minor and complex alignment issues with reliability.

    Your Path To A Beautiful, Straight Smile

    The simple, straightforward process in a nutshell:

    • We use a digital scanner to create a 3D “map” of your mouth.
    • Using the model and ClinCheck software, Dr. Pitner will create a custom treatment plan.
    • You get your tray aligners in sequenced sets and begin wearing them daily.
    • Depending on your treatment plan instructions, you switch to a new set of alignment trays every one to two weeks.
    • Your teeth gradually shift into alignment, creating your perfect dream smile!
