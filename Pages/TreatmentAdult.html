When Should My Kid See An Orthodontist

An early evaluation provides significant benefits for both you and your child. During your child’s initial evaluation, we will take photos and a panoramic x-ray. This helps us identify if your child has missing teeth, teeth not erupting properly, insufficient or excessive space for permanent teeth, or alignment issues. For the majority of children, no treatment is needed at this age. We will inform you if future treatment is likely. Your child will then have brief check-ups every six months to monitor their teeth's eruption and development. This process, provided at no charge to you, helps us determine the optimal time for your child to start treatment, ensuring the best and most efficient results.

Invisalign For Adults

For those seeking a less conspicuous orthodontic treatment without compromising effectiveness, Invisalign for adults at Pitner & Carroll Orthodontics is the ideal solution. Discover the advantages:

+ Almost Invisible:
Crafted from clear, high-quality plastic, Invisalign aligners are virtually undetectable, ensuring your orthodontic treatment remains your secret.
+ Maintains Professional Image:
Invisalign doesn’t compromise your professional look. Its discreet design ensures it doesn’t attract undue attention, allowing you to engage confidently in meetings, presentations, and social interactions without a second thought.
+ Flexibility:
Enjoy the freedom that comes with removable aligners. Whether it’s savoring your favorite meal or sticking to your oral hygiene routine, Invisalign doesn’t interrupt.
+ Customized Fit:
Experience the comfort of aligners tailored just for you. Each is meticulously designed to ensure precise movement throughout your treatment journey.
