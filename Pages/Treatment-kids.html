ORTHODONTIC TREATMENT FOR KIDS

Like Most Parents, You're Probably Wondering

WHEN SHOULD MY KID SEE AN ORTHODONTIST?

At Pitner and Carroll Orthodontics, We Recommend That All Children Be Evaluated At Age 7, But This Does Not Mean Your Child Will Need Braces In Second Grade Or Even In The Future. And You Don’t Need A Referral By A Dentist To Take Advantage Of This Free Evaluation.

When Should My Kid See An Orthodontist

An early evaluation provides significant benefits for both you and your child. During your child’s initial evaluation, we will take photos and a panoramic x-ray. This allows us to determine if your child has missing teeth, teeth that are not erupting properly, too little or too much space for permanent teeth, or issues with how the teeth fit together. For the vast majority of children, no treatment is needed at this age, and we will inform you if future treatment is likely. Your child will then be seen briefly every six months to monitor the eruption and development of their teeth. This process, offered at no charge to you, ensures we can identify the ideal time for your child to begin treatment, achieving the best and most efficient results.

5 Great Reasons To Choose Invisalign For Yourself

1. NO EMERGENCIES FROM BROKEN BRACES OR WIRES

With Invisalign, you don’t have to worry about the discomfort or emergencies that come with broken braces or wires.

2. FEWER AND LESS FREQUENT APPOINTMENTS

Invisalign requires fewer and less frequent visits to the orthodontist compared to traditional braces.

3. LESS DISCOMFORT

The smooth plastic aligners are comfortable to wear and cause less irritation to your mouth.

4. EASIER BRUSHING AND NO FOOD RESTRICTIONS

Since Invisalign aligners are removable, you can brush and floss your teeth as usual without any restrictions.

5. REPLACEMENT ALIGNERS AT NO EXTRA COST

Lost or broken aligners can be replaced at no additional cost, ensuring your treatment stays on track.
