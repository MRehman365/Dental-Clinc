Invisalign

Invisalign consists of a series of custom-made, removable aligners that gradually shift your teeth into the desired positions. Worn like mouthguards, these aligners are virtually invisible, so no one will know you're undergoing orthodontic treatment. Without metal brackets, the aligners are replaced approximately every two weeks. Since they're removable, there's no need to adjust your diet, and maintaining oral hygiene is easier. These are just a few reasons why adults love Invisalign treatment so much.

Clear Ceramic Braces

If you want to straighten your smile but don't qualify for Invisalign and prefer not to have hidden braces, clear ceramic braces are a perfect option! These braces are nearly identical to traditional braces in function, but they are made of clear ceramic. The translucent ceramic brackets blend with the natural color of your teeth, making them nearly invisible. This option is very popular among our adult orthodontic patients.
