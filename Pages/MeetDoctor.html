Meet Dr. Pitner & Dr. Carroll

SOUTH CAROLINA’S TOP INVISALIGN ORTHODONTISTS

Dr. Leslie Pitner

D.D.S., M.S.

Background & Education

I began my educational journey at Chapin High School, graduating in 1986 before pursuing my undergraduate degree at Williams College. There, I immersed myself in art, graduating with highest honors and earning membership in Phi Beta Kappa. My passion for art history led me to the University of Pennsylvania, where I completed a master’s degree in 1995. Following my artistic pursuits, I transitioned to dentistry, achieving distinction at the University of North Carolina School of Dentistry in 2002. During my time there, I was honored to join OKU, a prestigious dental honor society, and continued on to earn my Master of Science in Orthodontics in 2005, cementing my dedication to the field

In addition to my technical training in dentistry and orthodontics, I pursued a distinctive master’s degree program in positive psychology at the University of Pennsylvania in 2007. This program focuses on fostering personal growth and happiness in human development, shifting the focus from pathology to overall well-being and fulfillment.
