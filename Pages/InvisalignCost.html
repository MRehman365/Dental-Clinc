Invisalign Cost & Process

The Process Of Developing Your Custom-Made Invisalign Braces Is Easy And Straightforward!

1

We will use a scanner to make a 3D model of your teeth.

2

Based on the model, we will create a treatment plan that our team will use as our guide to guide your teeth into perfect alignment.

3

You’ll receive a set of clear plastic aligners (the Invisalign braces). The number of aligners in the set depends on your individual needs – for some clients, it’s as few as 5-6, and for others, it can be up to 70 or more pairs.

4

You’ll then wear each pair of plastic aligners for one week, then switch to the next pair the following week.

5

Gradually, the aligners will shift your teeth to create a beautiful, straight smile!
