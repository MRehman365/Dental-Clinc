Smiles By Design

Confident Smiles Require More Than Just “Straight” Teeth.

SMILE ARC

Natural, aesthetic smiles often align with the contour of the lips. While many orthodontists prioritize perfectly straight teeth, the true secret to a better smile lies in harmonizing the bottom edge of your upper teeth with the natural curve of your mouth.

SMILE VME

Smile VME corrects the "Gummy Smile" by adjusting the bite to position the gums less prominently.

SMILE WIDTH

Research indicates that a broader smile is more favorable. We aim to showcase a maximum number of teeth, ensuring visibility from front to back, while highlighting each individual tooth.

Early Evaluation

Early Evaluation

Invisalign consists of custom-made, removable aligners designed to gradually reposition your teeth. These aligners are worn like mouthguards over your teeth and are virtually invisible, making them discreet during wear.

Invisalign

Invisalign

Invisalign consists of a series of customized, removable aligners that gently shift your teeth into the desired positions. These aligners are worn over your teeth like mouthguards and are nearly invisible to the naked eye.
